Project context

  • Project type : 2d adventure video game designed in pixel art
  • Software used : Visual Studio 2019 and Unity 2019.4.15f1 LTS
  • Plateforms : Windows and Mac
  • Language : C#

Game story

Following the creation of an exceptional articial intelligence named GAIA (Générateur Atomique d'Intelligence Artificielle in French), scientists lost its control.
Taking advantage of the situation, GAIA inflitrated all electronics devices in the world and mesmerized all humans using this type of equipment.
Some people were not infected for an unknown reason.
Among these survivors, a young prodigy programmer called Marvin try to find out how to end this cataclysm.
Equipped with his smart phone and orginal weapons, he will brave Gaia's creations in a transformed world.

Content details

Hero

Prodigy programmer in his early twenties called Marvin (in memory of Marvin Lee Minsky, one of the Artifical Intelligence pioneers).

Goal

Destroy mother AI who controls electronic devices and humans in order to restore freedom to Earth inhabitants.

Levels

A total of nine levels will be available when the game will be finished. In addition, a headquarters allowing you to change levels and make improvements on your stuff will be created.

The game scenario has been completely written and remains available on request (as part of an interview for a position or an internship).

Ennemis

Different types of enemy robots
Infected humans (simple infection up to total transformation with bio-mechanic parts)
For each boss fight, enemy will be totally different.

Ally

Merchants: sell upgrades (weapons, phone apps and armor)
Survivors: Temporary boosts or help with puzzles.

Decorations level

The decorations will be as diverse as possible: urban, forest, school, factory, headquarters in sewers, laboratory, shopping center.

Game mechanics

Melee, ranged weapons, traps, zone damage, puzzles, secret passages, mobile decors.

Inspirations

Watchdogs, Splinter Cell, Hitman, the Escapist.
